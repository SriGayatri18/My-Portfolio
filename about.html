About Me

Hello! I'm Sri Gayatri Nandikanti, currently pursuing a Bachelor of Technology in Information Technology at Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology. I have a strong passion for technology and love to create projects that solve real-world problems.

My journey in the tech world has been fueled by curiosity and a desire to innovate. I have completed various projects including an Alarm Clock using Python, a YouTube Transcript Summarizer using Python and Natural Language Processing, a Sports Event Management System using HTML, CSS, XAMPP, Apache, PHP, and MySQL, and a Real-time Chat Application using MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, and Node.js.

I have also achieved 3rd Prize at Chaitanyam Ideathon, received an ELITE certification in Python computing from NPTEL, and completed certifications in Data Structures and Algorithms and Data Science Foundation.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy exploring new technologies, reading about the latest trends in the tech industry, and engaging in hackathons and coding competitions. I am always eager to learn and grow, and I look forward to the exciting opportunities that the future holds.
